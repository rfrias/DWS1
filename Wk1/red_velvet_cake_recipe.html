Red Velvet Cake

Submitted by: Ramon Rafael G. Frias IV

Prep time: 25 minutes

Cook time: 30 minutes

Recipe was acquired from allrecipes.com

This classic red velvet layer cake is made tender with buttermilk. It's topped of with a fluffy cooked white icing.-allrecipes.com

Ingredients

Cake:

  • ½C Shortening
  • 1½C White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 4tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 4tbsp Red Food Coloring
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1C buttermilk
  • 2½C All-Purpose Flour
  • 1½tsp Baking Soda
  • 1tbsp Distilled White Vinegar/li>

Icing:

  • 5tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  • 1C Milk
  • 1C White Sugar
  • 1C Butter (Room Temperature)
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and grease two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Mix ½C shortening and 1½C white sugar until it becomes light and fluffy. Add the 2 eggs and mix well.
  3. Mix 2tbsp cocoa powder and 4tbsp red food coloring until all the cocoa powder is mixed well together with the red food coloring and has a paste-like texture; Add to mixture above. Mix 1tsp salt, 1 tsp vanilla exract, and 1C buttermilk together. Add the flour to the batter, alternating with the milk mixture, mixing just until incorporated well. Mix 1½tsp baking soda and 1tbsp distilled white vinegar and gently fold into cake batter. Dont beat or stir the batter after this point.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pans and put the baking pans in the oven. Bake for about 30 minutes or until a tester is inserted into the cake and comes out clean. After the cake has been successfully baked, cool the cake completely using a wire rack.
  5. Icing: Cook 5tbsp of all-purpose flour and 1C milk over low heat until it becomes thick (stir constantly). After it becomes thick, let it cool completely. While the mixture is cooling, beat 1C white sugar, 1C butter (room temperature), and 1tsp vanilla extract until it becomes light and fluffy. Add the mixture that you set aside earlier to cool and mix until frosting has good consistency for spreading. Add the frosting to the cake layers.
